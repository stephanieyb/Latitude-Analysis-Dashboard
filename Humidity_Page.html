How Does Latitude Affects Humidity?


Humidityis a term used to describe the amount of water vapor present in air. Humidity indicates the likelihood for precipitation, dew, or fog to be present. Humidity can be measured in several ways, but relative humidity (RH) is the most common. In order to understand RH, it is helpful to first understand absolute humidity.

Absolute humidity is the mass of water vapor divided by the mass of dry air in a volume of air at a given temperature. The hotter the air is, the more water it can contain.

Relative humidity is the ratio of the current absolute humidity to the highest possible absolute humidity.
