How Does Latitude Affects Wind Speed?


Wind is the movement of air in any direction. It is created when air moves from areas of high pressure toward that of low. Seasonal temperature changes and the Earth’s rotation also affect wind speed and direction.

Four main factors affect wind speed are temperature, air pressure, centripetal acceleration, Earth’s rotation. The heating of the Earth's surface by the sun is the force creating the circulation.
